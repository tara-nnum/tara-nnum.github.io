Biography

I am Tarannum Tabassum Islam , a Hydroinformatics graduate combining data science, hydrological modelling, and climate analytics to improve decision-making for sustainable water systems. My work bridges hydrodynamics, groundwater, and AI-based environmental modelling, with a focus on actionable insights for water resilience and climate adaptation.

I completed my Erasmus Mundus MSc in Hydroinformatics & Water Management (EUROAQUAE+) and am currently based in Germany, pursuing research projects related to hydrological data pipelines, causal ML, and climate-driven aquaculture modelling.
